About the club

The Cream Hill Lake Association has brought Cornwall families together on the water since 1891. Our mission is the preservation of the natural beauty of Cream Hill Lake and the maintenance of a clubhouse for members — offering boating, swimming, tennis, and good summer gatherings on the water.
