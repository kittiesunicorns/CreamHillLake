Special circumstances

Installment plan

Members may request to pay their January bill in installments. To qualify:

  • Contact the Secretary to request installment payment.
  • Send one-third of your total bill, postmarked on or before the Preliminary Meeting (fourth Saturday in June).
  • All installments must be completed by November 1.
  • Failure to complete installments by November 1 will result in membership termination.

New member timing

New members are typically invited in May, June, and July as spaces become available.

  • Members invited after August 1 still pay dues for the current year, but the initiation fee is deferred to the following year.
  • Declining an invitation voids your application — the next approved member on the waitlist will be offered the spot and you must start the application process again.
Late fees & membership termination

If a member fails to pay their bill and does not contact the Secretary to make payment arrangements by the date of the Preliminary Meeting, the membership will be terminated.

Late fees are 10% per month on unpaid balances:

  • January invoices: late fees begin June 1
  • November invoices: late fees begin February 1
