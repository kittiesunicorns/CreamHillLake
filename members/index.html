Committees

Chair: Ronnie Lizana  |  Members: Jeff Ward, Noah Noyes, Joe DoRocco, Chris Sampson, Josh Tyson, Dana Saccardi, Phill West, Susan Saccardi, Todd Lunderborg

  • Develop and follow a maintenance checklist — driveway, beach, buildings, seasonal setup and teardown.
  • Weekly check-in with staff to adjust the upcoming week's schedule.
  • Provide a yearly repairs list to the Executive Committee by November 1.
  • Coordinate Clean-Up Day with House Operations.
  • Provide contractor suggestions and cost estimates for lawn, driveway, building, and court maintenance.
  • Hire approved contractors and supervise their work.
  • Develop a 5-year capital plan for major improvements.
  • Report to membership at the Preliminary and Annual Meetings.

Chair: Dan Horan  |  Members: Martha Bruehl, Elizabeth England, Eileen Gargan, Phill West, Ingrid Ellen, David Cavalier

  • Prepares a slate of Officers and Executive Committee members.
  • Presents the slate at the Preliminary Meeting.
  • Must have at least 5 members, one of whom is on the Executive Committee.
  • Reports at the Preliminary and Annual Meetings.

Chair: Susie Ott  |  Members: Heather Abrams, Ingrid Ellen, Jacque Schiller, Bente Busby

  • Gathers nomination letters and reviews applications each spring.
  • Submits approved applications to the Secretary.
  • Must have at least 5 members. May not sponsor or second nominations.
  • Meets membership nominees as part of the application process.
  • Helps monitor guest and non-member use of CHLA.
  • Reports at the Preliminary and Annual Meetings.

Co-Chairs: Annette Doskow, Katherine Scoville

  • Coordinates lifeguard hiring — advertising, interviewing, certification, and scheduling.
  • Creates and manages waterfront safety policies; communicates them to staff and members.
  • Develops annual equipment budget; submits supply needs to the Executive Committee.
  • Supervises lifeguards daily; evaluates mid-season; oversees end-of-season cleanup.
  • Serves as member liaison for waterfront safety questions.
  • Coordinates with Building & Grounds for waterfront needs and hazard remediation.
  • Reports at the Preliminary and Annual Meetings.

Chair: Kate Green  |  Members: Jane Barnet, Ben Lesch, Tory Estern Jadow, Joyce Keisman, Todd Piker, Shoshana Pinedo, Eileen Gargan

  • Plans and oversees all tennis programming — lessons, clinics, doubles, tournaments, round robins, mixers, and inter-clubs.
  • Runs the end-of-summer tournament and procures trophies.
  • Coordinates with Building & Grounds for court maintenance and with Entertainment for event refreshments.
  • Coordinates volunteers for seasonal court setup and teardown (nets, windscreens, awning).
  • Manages the court reservation program.
  • Reviews and recommends updates to tennis rules.
  • Reports at the Preliminary and Annual Meetings.

Co-Chairs: Melissa Silvetti, Kate Ward  |  Members: Eiseley Kotchoubey, Kate Reynolds

  • Plans and oversees social, culinary, and educational programming.
  • Schedules events in coordination with the Executive Committee, Waterfront, and Tennis.
  • Organizes paid staff or volunteers for each event; maintains a tent committee.
  • Purchases event supplies; coordinates sign-ups with the Secretary/Treasurer.
  • Reports at the Preliminary and Annual Meetings.

Chair: Vacant — volunteers welcome!

  • Hires and supervises housekeeping staff.
  • Maintains Lost & Found and disposes of unclaimed items regularly.
  • Keeps the supply closet stocked with cleaning supplies, paper goods, and soap.
  • Maintains the ping pong table and equipment.
  • Coordinates Clean-Up Day with Building & Grounds.
  • Maintains bulletin boards; monitors kitchen and building cleanliness.
  • Makes recommendations for Clubhouse purchases to the Executive Committee.
  • Reports at the Preliminary and Annual Meetings.

Chair: Todd Lunderborg  |  Members: Caspar Luard, Julie Beglin, Spencer Compton, Alison Schweizer, Jim Stewart

  • Drafts the annual operating budget for Executive Committee approval.
  • Considers future operating and capital financial needs; recommends plans to the Executive Committee.
